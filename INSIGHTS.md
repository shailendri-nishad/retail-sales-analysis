# Sales Insights - Key Findings

## Overall Performance

Between 2014 and 2017, the business generated:

- Total Sales: $2.30M
- Total Profit: $286K
- Profit Margin: 12.5%
- Total Orders: 5,009

The business showed strong revenue growth over the four-year period.

## Regional Sales Analysis

The West region generated the highest sales, followed closely by the East region.

- West: $725K
- East: $679K
- Central: $501K
- South: $392K

West and East together contribute a major share of total revenue.

## Top Performing States

California is the highest-performing state, followed by New York.

- California: $457.69K
- New York: $310.88K
- Texas: $170.19K
- Washington: $138.64K
- Pennsylvania: $116.51K

Sales are concentrated in a few high-performing states.

## Customer Segment Analysis

The Consumer segment contributes the largest share of revenue.

- Consumer: 50.56% ($1.16M)
- Corporate: 30.74% ($0.71M)
- Home Office: 18.70% ($0.43M)

Consumer customers are the primary revenue drivers.

## Product Performance

Top-selling sub-categories:

- Phones: $330K
- Chairs: $328K
- Storage: $224K
- Tables: $207K
- Binders: $203K

Phones and Chairs are the strongest-performing product groups.

## Sales Trend

Annual sales performance:

- 2014: $484K
- 2015: $471K
- 2016: $609K
- 2017: $733K

2017 recorded the highest sales, growing by approximately 20.4% compared to 2016.

## Opportunities for Growth

- Improve sales performance in Central and South regions.
- Expand successful strategies from the West region.
- Increase focus on high-performing product categories.
- Monitor profit margins while driving revenue growth.

## Recommendations

1. Strengthen expansion efforts in underperforming regions.
2. Continue promoting top-selling products such as Phones and Chairs.
3. Analyze customer purchasing patterns to improve retention.
4. Monitor discount strategies to protect profitability.
5. Use high-performing states as benchmarks for other markets.
